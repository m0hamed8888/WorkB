PIZZA SAUCE

Wholesale Pizza Sauce for Industrial & Horeca applications. Consistent viscosity, rich flavor, and optimized for high-temperature ovens.

WHY CHOOSE SAPORINA'S PIZZA SAUCE?

Engineered for high-performance kitchens, our sauce delivers the perfect balance of sweetness and acidity. We ensure consistent viscosity and zero syneresis (water separation), making it the ideal base for professional pizza applications.

100% Natural

Free from GMOs, artificial colors, and preservatives. Just pure tomato goodness.

24h Harvest

Tomatoes are processed within 24 hours of harvest to lock in maximum freshness.

Advanced Tech

State-of-the-art thermal processing ensures consistent texture and food safety.

STORAGE & LOGISTICS

Shelf Life: 24 months from the date of production.

Storage Conditions: Store in a cool, dry place (< 25°C Recommended) away from direct sunlight and humidity (< 65% Relative humidity).

After Opening: Transfer to a non-metallic container. Refrigerate at 4°C. Consume within 5 days.

Logistics: Palletized or Loose Loaded options available. Shipped in 20ft or 40ft Dry Containers.
