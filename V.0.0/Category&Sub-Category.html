Premium Wholesale Tomato Products from Egypt

Saporina specializes in the manufacturing and export of high-quality tomato derivatives sourced directly from Egypt's fertile lands. Our range—including Cold & Hot Break Tomato Paste, Pizza Sauce, and Passata—is engineered for the Retail, Food Service (HORECA), and Industrial sectors. We guarantee consistent Brix levels, vibrant color, and optimal viscosity in every batch.

We offer flexible packaging solutions ranging from Aseptic Bags in Steel Drums (220lt) for industrial reprocessing to branded canned retail sizes. Committed to international food safety standards (ISO & HACCP), Saporina is your trusted partner for Private Label (OEM) manufacturing, offering recipe adaptation and end-to-end logistical support for global markets.
